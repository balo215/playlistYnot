About us

What is YNot?

YNot is a website for you to enjoy video playlists connected with friends and strangers all over the internet. You can create your own playlists or search and join already existant ones.

The creating team!

YNot was created by a small group of dreamers who believe in the power of music and channeled the power of the internet to connect people all over the world. Their goal is to achieve the domination of basement-dwellers by keeping them in front of their computers, innocently creating profit by ads and publicity.
